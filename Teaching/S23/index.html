Potential papers for student presentations

Presentations may be 30-50 minutes in length, and should focus on main ideas from a paper. Since there will not be time to give many detailed proofs, presentations should outline key results and key steps in the argument.

  1. Duminil-Copin, Smirnov, The connective constant of the honeycomb lattice
  2. Gywnne, Miller, Xun, Almost sure multifractal spectrum of SLE
  3. Alberts, Byun, Kang, Makarov, Pole dynamics and an integral of motion for multiple SLE(0)
